# How to request account deletion in Word Battle app

Effective date: March 31, 2026

The Word Battle app retains:
- the email address you provided
- a hash of your password
- your submitted Word results
- messages you sent
- your scores and participations in tournaments
- tournaments you've created

To request your account deletion please email 

- heavyatomstudios AT gmail DOT com

with the subject "Word Battle App Account Deletion Request"
and the body containing the email address you used to sign up for the account.
